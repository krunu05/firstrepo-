I Phone 16 Pro

The iPhone 16 Pro features a 6.3-inch Super Retina XDR display, a titanium design, and the A18 Pro chip. It boasts a Pro camera system with a 48MP main sensor, and the ability to record 4K video at up to 120fps in Dolby Vision. It also integrates with Apple Intelligence, a suite of AI features, and includes a customizable Action button.

Key Features:

  • Display: 6.3-inch Super Retina XDR display, ProMotion technology with adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz.


  • Design: Titanium design with a textured matte glass back.


  • Cameras: Pro camera system with 48MP main sensor, 5x optical zoom, and 4K video recording up to 120fps in Dolby Vision.
