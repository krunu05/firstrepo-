Royal Enfield Continental GT 650


The Royal Enfield Continental GT 650 is a cafe racer-style motorcycle powered by a 648cc parallel-twin engine, producing 47 bhp and 52 Nm of torque. It features a classic design with a sculpted fuel tank, rearset footrests, and a bump stop seat, offering a sporty riding position. The bike also includes a double-cradle frame, twin rear shock absorbers, and disc brakes with dual-channel ABS.



    Key Details

  • Engine: 648cc parallel-twin, air/oil-cooled.
  • Power: 47 bhp @ 7250 rpm.
  • Torque: 52 Nm @ 5250 rpm.
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual.
  • Weight: 214 kg (kerb).
