Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj

Sambhaji Maharaj, born on May 14, 1657, was the eldest son of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of the Maratha Empire. He became the second Chhatrapati of the Marathas after his father's death in 1680. Sambhaji was a skilled warrior, a brilliant strategist, and a learned scholar, fluent in multiple languages. He played a crucial role in expanding and defending the Maratha kingdom against the Mughal Empire and other adversaries. Early Life and Education: Sambhaji received a rigorous education in military strategy, administration, and various languages, including Sanskrit, Hindi, Marathi, and Persian.
He was known for his intellectual pursuits and authored the Sanskrit text "Budhbhushanam".
His mother, Saibai, passed away when he was young, and he was later raised by his grandmother, Jijabai.

Birth Day :- 14 May 1657

Death Day :- 11th March 1689
