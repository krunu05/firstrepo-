I Phone 16 Pro Max

The iPhone 16 Pro Max features a 6.9-inch Super Retina XDR display, a titanium design, and the A18 Pro chip for enhanced performance. It includes a pro camera system with a 48MP ultra-wide camera and 5x optical zoom. Other notable features include Wi-Fi 7, fast USB-C charging, and up to 33 hours of video playback. It's available in Black Titanium, White Titanium, Natural Titanium, and Desert Titanium finishes.

Key Features:

  • Display: 6.9-inch (diagonal) all-screen OLED display with rounded corners. 2868x1320-pixel resolution at 460 ppi. Dynamic Island and Always-On display. ProMotion technology with adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz. HDR display, True Tone, and wide color (P3) support. 2,000,000:1 contrast ratio (typical). 1,000 nits max brightness (typical); 1,600 nits peak brightness (HDR); 2,000 nits peak brightness (outdoor).


  • Performance: A18 Pro chip. Internal design improvements for better heat dissipation, enabling up to 20% better sustained performance compared to iPhone 15 Pro. Up to 33 hours of video playback.


  • Cameras: Pro camera system with a 48MP ultra-wide camera. 5x optical zoom. 4K Dolby Vision video recording at various frame rates (24, 25, 30, 60, 100, or 120 fps).
