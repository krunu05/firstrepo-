Gir Somnath is a district in Gujarat, India, known for the Somnath Temple, a revered Jyotirlinga shrine, and the Gir Forest, the only natural habitat of Asiatic lions. The district was formed in 2013, carved out from Junagadh, with Veraval as its headquarters.
