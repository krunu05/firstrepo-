I Phone 15 Pro

The iPhone 15 pro, released in September 2023, features a 6.1-inch Super Retina display, an A16 Bionic chip, and a dual-camera system with a 48MP main camera. It also includes USB-C connectivity, supports wireless charging, and is rated IP68 for water and dust resistance. The iPhone 15 is available in Black, Blue, Green, Pink, and Yellow.

Key Features:

  • Display: 6.1-inch Super Retina display with a resolution of 1179x2556 pixels and 460 ppi.


  • Processor: A15 Bionic chip.


  • Cameras: Dual-camera system with a 48MP main camera and a 12MP ultrawide camera.
