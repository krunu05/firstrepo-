I Phone 16

The iPhone 16 series introduces Apple Intelligence, a suite of AI-powered features, along with a new Camera Control button and a more powerful A18 chip. Other notable upgrades include enhanced camera capabilities with 48MP Fusion camera and spatial photo and video capture, plus new Photographic Styles. The Pro models also feature a 12MP telephoto lens with 5x optical zoom.

Key Features:

  • Display: 6.3-inch Super Retina XDR display, ProMotion technology with adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz.


  • Performance: A18 chip: Provides a significant performance boost and improved power efficiency. Larger displays: The Pro models have larger displays,


  • Cameras: 48MP Fusion camera: Allows for high-resolution photos and 2x optical zoom. Spatial Video: Enables capturing spatial photos and videos for viewing on compatible devices. Ultra Wide camera: Can capture macro photos and wider scenes. New Camera Control button: Provides quick access to camera functions, including launching the camera, taking photos, and starting videos, says Boy Genius Report. Photographic Styles: Offers various digital filters to customize the look and feel of your photos.
