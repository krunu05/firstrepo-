Vir Hamirji Gohil

Veer Hamirji Gohil was a 16th-century Rajput chieftain from Lathi, Gujarat, who is renowned for his valiant defense of the Somnath Temple against invaders. He is celebrated for sacrificing his life in 1299 AD to protect the temple from an attack by forces of the Delhi Sultanate, led by Alauddin Khilji's general, Nusrat Khan according to X users. His cenotaph stands at the entrance of the Somnath Temple as a memorial to his courage.
