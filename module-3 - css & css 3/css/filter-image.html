Vir Hamirji

Veer Hamirji Gohil was a 16-year-old Rajput chieftain from Lathi, Gujarat, who sacrificed himself in 1299 AD while defending the Somnath temple against an invasion led by a general of Alauddin Khilji. He is remembered for his bravery and devotion to the temple, with a memorial stone at its entrance.
